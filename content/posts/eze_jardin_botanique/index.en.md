## 🥾 Eze - From the train station to the botanical garden

Welcome to Eze! Today, I’m taking you on a super accessible stroll: from the platforms of the little Eze-sur-Mer train station all the way up to the botanical garden, perched above like a hidden treasure. This is the kind of hike that’s perfect for curious Sunday explorers—treat yourself to a stunning panorama of the Mediterranean, without having to break a sweat like a marathon runner.

No need to be a compass pro or have fancy gear here. We’ll simply make our way up the legendary Nietzsche Trail, surrounded by the scent of pine and moments of peaceful reflection. The finish line? Pure magic: a cute medieval village that’s just right for a coffee break, plus cacti that look straight out of a Western—with the blue sea as your backdrop.

So lace up your sneakers, grab a water bottle, and let yourself be guided... You’ll see, outings like this are pure happiness and guaranteed vacation vibes!
