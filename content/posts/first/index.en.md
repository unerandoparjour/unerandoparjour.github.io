## 💡 Hiking 101 (To look smart at parties)

Before you lace up your boots, here is a quick breakdown of French trail markings. It's basically the rules of the road for trails, but way cooler! 🗺️👇

* **GR® (Grande Randonnée):** The famous long-distance treks for full-on adventurer mode. 🧭
  * *Marker :* A **white + red** stripe ⬜🟥 (impossible to miss!)

  Example : [Le fameux GR 20](https://www.ffrandonnee.fr/randonner/sentiers/gr-20-a-travers-la-montagne-corse)

* **GRP® (Grande Randonnée de Pays):** Regional loops designed to let you discover a specific area inside out. 🔁
  * *Marker :* A **yellow + red** mix 🟨🟥

  Example : [GR® de pays Lou Camin Nissart](https://alpes-maritimes.ffrandonnee.fr/post/camin-nissart/)


* **PR (Promenade et Randonnée):** Short day-hikes, perfect for a quick getaway (or a sunset walk after work!). 🎒
  * *Marker :* A single **yellow** stripe 🟨

  Example : [Eze - Mont Bastide](https://www.ffrandonnee.fr/randonner/idees-rando/vos-parcours-avec-l-appli-ma-rando?mb_page=circuit&mb_id=359810&mb_titre=eze-mont-bastide)

### 🛡️ What does the "Official Trail" label mean?

When you follow these markers, you can hike stress-free. Why? Because they are:

* 🎨 **Perfectly marked:** No need to have the internal compass of a homing pigeon.
* 🛠️ **Maintained to perfection:** No giant brambles blocking your path.
* 📜 **Certified:** Officially approved so you don't run into any nasty surprises.

Honestly, don't let big names like "Grande Randonnée" intimidate you. Most of the time, it’s way more accessible than you think!

## 🛠️ A few must-have tools and websites

To plan your outings without the headache, here is my magic toolbox. These are tried-and-true favorites to map your route, check the terrain, and avoid any hassle! 🧭👇

* **[Explore Nice Côte d'Azur](https://www.explorenicecotedazur.com/) :** The ultimate local site focused entirely on our beautiful region. Perfect for finding local gems and quick getaway ideas right in our backyard. 🌊☀️
* **[MaRando](https://www.ffrandonnee.fr/randonner/idees-rando/vos-parcours-avec-l-appli-ma-rando) :** The official app from the French Hiking Federation (FFRandonnée). It’s the bible for certified itineraries! The little bonus? They have a great widget on their website, which is super handy if you prefer planning your route on a big computer screen rather than your phone. 📱💻
* **[AllTrails](https://www.alltrails.com/fr) :** The ultimate community-driven app. Its biggest strength is the super active user base—you get tons of recent reviews on every trail page, letting you know if a path is clear or if there’s been a recent rockslide. 💬🎒
* **[Visorando](https://www.visorando.com/randonnee-nice.html) :** Another heavyweight in the hiking world! The site and app are packed with highly detailed route guides, precise maps, and photos to help you visualize the trip. It’s the perfect tool to find trails around Nice based on your level, duration, or the vibe you're looking for (coastal breeze, fresh forest air, or alpine peaks). 🌲🏔️
* **[Google Earth](https://www.google.com/earth/about/versions/) (et Google Maps) :** My secret weapon for previewing trails in 3D before heading out! I always check the satellite view before hitting the road. Why? To make sure there are actually trees and greenery along the way... Just to avoid the classic trap of walking for 2 hours under a scorching sun on asphalt or right through a concrete jungle! 🌍🔎
