## 🥾 Gilette - Fougassières - La Clave

This hike between Gilette, the Fougassières and La Clave offers a beautiful immersion into the Niçois hinterland. The itinerary alternates forest tracks, balconies and more wild passages, with stunning views over the surrounding valleys. Between perched villages, restanques and Mediterranean landscapes, this loop constitutes an enjoyable outing to discover preserved rural heritage in a quiet and authentic atmosphere.
