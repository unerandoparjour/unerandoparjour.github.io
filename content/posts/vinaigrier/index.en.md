## 🥾 Parc du Vinaigrier Nice

Located on the hills of Nice, the Vinaigrier Park offers a refreshing walk between olive groves, restanques and spectacular views over the Baie des Anges. This itinerary, accessible to all, snakes through preserved Mediterranean vegetation, where tranquility reigns far from urban agitation. Along the trail, beautiful views open up over Nice, the port and the sea, punctuated by the discovery of old vineyards and typical flora of the Nice hills. Perfect for a contemplative pause or an evening hike, this park constitutes an unexpected haven of greenery just a few steps from the city.
